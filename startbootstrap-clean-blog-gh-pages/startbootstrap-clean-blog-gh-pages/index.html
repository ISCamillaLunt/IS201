Fun Facts about Italy

  • Most musical terms are written in Italian because most early composers from the Renaissance era were Italian.
  • Italy contains three active volcanos, Etna, Stromboli, and Vesuvius.
  • Ballet originated in Italy during the 15th century.
  • Many designer brands come from Italy. These in include Valentino, Fendi, Prada, Versace, Gucci, and Armani.
